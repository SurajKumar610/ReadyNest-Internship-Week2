# SIGHTFILL – PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

# Project Name

Sightfill

# Tagline

AI-Powered Customer Insights, Analytics, Forecasting & Recommendation Platform

---

# 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Sightfill is a full-stack SaaS-based Customer Insights and Recommendation Platform that transforms raw sales datasets into actionable business intelligence.

Users can upload sales data and automatically generate:

* Data Cleaning & Preparation
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Customer Analytics
* Product Analytics
* Regional Analytics
* Customer Segmentation
* Predictive Analytics
* AI-Powered Insights
* Business Recommendations
* Executive Reports

The platform should provide enterprise-grade dashboards, AI-powered analytics, downloadable reports, and predictive intelligence through a modern web application.

Primary business questions answered:

* Who are our best customers?
* Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
* Which regions are most profitable?
* Which customers are likely to churn?
* What products should be promoted?
* What business actions should be taken to increase growth?

The system must be production-ready, deployment-ready, GitHub-ready, and suitable for portfolio, placement, internship, and final-year project demonstrations.
